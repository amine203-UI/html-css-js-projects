
Contactly -  Modern Contact Book

Contactly is a modern and stylish contact book application that allows you to easily manage your personal and professional contacts. With a futuristic design, smooth animations, and useful features like editing, searching, and saving data locally, Contactly makes organizing contacts simple and enjoyable.

 
 
Features

Add Contacts – Save full name, phone number, email, job, and profile image.

Edit Profile & Contacts – Update your own profile or any contact through an elegant popup form.

Delete Contacts – Remove a contact with confirmation to avoid mistakes.

Search Contacts – Quickly find contacts by name, email, or phone.

Contact Counter – Displays the total number of saved contacts.

Automatic Timestamps – Each new contact is automatically saved with the date and time.

Notifications –

 Green message when a contact is added.

 Blue message when a contact is updated.

 Red message when a contact is deleted.

Empty State Handling – Shows “Sorry, the contact book is empty” when no contacts are stored.

Dark/Light Mode – Switch seamlessly between black (dark) and white (light) themes.

Local Storage – All contacts are stored in the browser’s local storage, so your data remains safe even after refreshing.

Unlimited Contacts – Add as many contacts as you want.

Modern Design – Black blurred background, white frosted sections, and smooth animations.



 Tech Stack

HTML5 – Structure

CSS3 (with animations & blur effects) – Styling

JavaScript (Vanilla) – Functionality & Local Storage
